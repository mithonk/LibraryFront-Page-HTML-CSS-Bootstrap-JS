Briefly About Us


The origin of Mitho Library can be traced back to May 1997, when the Library was formally established in Colombo by Mitho Foundation. The Mitho Library plays a vital role in the collection development and dissemination of scientific and technical information to meet the present and future needs of the Readers.

At present, this Library is helping to over 20,000 readers. Library provides quality services and access to information. It also offers teaching and learning environment by providing a wide-ranging knowledge with diverse resources. also, this library is providing many online e-services for Readers.

We invite you to visit the Mitho library in order to enjoy the wealth of resources available in our library.

Message from the Librarian


Welcome to the Mitho Library which is one of the services of Mitho Foundation. The Mission of the Mitho library is to underpin the Readers’ vision and mission in building synergies between information, innovation, education and research through an excellent library and information services.

I invite you to visit the Mitho library in order to enjoy the wealth of resources available in our library, remain connected to us by exploring our website, where you will find an increasing number of digital resources available without restrictions on their use.

Please let us know of ways in which we could improve or expand upon our services.
